Roteiro · Curta-metragem 2025

Water & Weed

Dois irmãos, um cais abandonado, um ritual e uma conversa difícil. Roteiro, direção, atuação e edição — de ponta a ponta.

She sighs. JANE "I don't wanna talk about dad". ETHAN "He's gone, man. It's nobody's fault, there's nothing we could have done…" JANE "Shut the fuck up, dude". He doesn't engage as quickly as before. Ethan takes his time, smoking his cigarette, letting himself enjoy it before he finally speaks. ETHAN "You were the one who fucking taught me: don't hold up to things, don't let them consume you, talk about them. Now old man's gone, you don't wanna talk about it". JANE "Last time I saw him, we had a fight". Silence. Then— ETHAN "I know. He told me". Her voice breaks— JANE "Why?" ETHAN "He was ashamed of himself?" JANE "Bullshit". He snorts. ETHAN "Everything's bullshit, Jane. You gotta decide what kind of bullshit to believe in". She gets up, frustrated with his coach's speech. She decides to get upstairs to the pier. We follow her steps, the river is now more visible. There’s a dystopian aspect to the place itself, residues of the last flood still there in contrast with the abandoned industrial machinery. The joint? Still with her.
